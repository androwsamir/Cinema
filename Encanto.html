Encanto


  • Genre: Comedy
                 Fantasy

  • Running Time: 145 min

  • Release Date: November 24, 2021

  • Starring: Stephanie Beatriz

  • Language: English

  • Subtitle: Arabic





   Encanto is about a family called the Madrigals, who
live in an enchanted town in the mountains of Colombia. Every
child but one in their magical house has been granted a unique
ability such as strength or healing powers. Mirabel, the only
ordinary child, discovers that the magic is under threat and
she may be the only one able to save it.
