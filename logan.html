BLACK WIDOW










  • Genre: Action

  • Director: James Mangold

  • Running Time: 2h 21m

  • Release Date: 20 May 2021

  • Starring: Hugh Jackman
                   Patrick Stewart
                   Richard E. Grant
                   Boyd Holbrook
                   Stephen Merchant
                   Dafne Keen

  • Language: English

  • Subtitle: Arabic





   In the near future, a weary Logan (Hugh Jackman)
cares for an ailing Professor X (Patrick Stewart) at a
remote outpost on the Mexican border. His plan to hide
from the outside world gets upended when he meets a
young mutant (Dafne Keen) who is very much like him.
Logan must now protect the girl and battle the dark
forces that want to capture her.
